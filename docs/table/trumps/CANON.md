## 1. Core Identity

`CANON` is a transmission trump.

It does not search the whole deck.
It does not select from a menu.
It does not stop after one draw.

It opens a line
and continues drawing
while the revealed top card belongs to the same canon.

Short formula:

```text
reveal top
if it carries ‚ėį or ‚ėĶ, draw it and continue
otherwise stop
```

## 2. Reading

`‚ėĶ ENCODE` here is not swap alone.

```text

```

`‚ėį CONNECT` here is not ordinary assembly.

This is not:

## 3. Core Law

When `CANON` becomes known:

1. Reveal the top card of `deck`.
2. If the revealed card is a trump, stop the `CANON` chain and resolve that trump by ordinary trump draw law.
3. If the revealed card is a minor card carrying `‚ėį` or `‚ėĶ`, draw it into `hand` and repeat from step `1`.
4. Otherwise stop. The revealed card remains revealed on top of `deck`.
5. After `CANON` fully resolves, it follows ordinary trump ecology.

Short formula:

```text
reveal top
trump -> ordinary trump law, CANON ends
minor with ‚ėį or ‚ėĶ -> hand, continue
other minor -> remains revealed, stop
```

The revealed top card left by `CANON`
counts as visible for later visibility-based effects.

## 4. Why The Card Reveals First

`CANON` does not draw blindly.

It reveals the top card first
because continuation depends on public recognition
of whether the line still holds.

This matters.

The chain is not:

```text
draw and secretly check
```

It is:

```text
the next card is shown
the line is confirmed or broken
before admission
```

Short formula:

```text
continuation is recognized before admission
```

## 5. Trump Interruption

If the revealed top card is a trump,
`CANON` does not take it into `hand`.

Instead:

- the draw line burns
- the trump follows ordinary trump draw law
- `CANON` stops immediately

This is intentional.

`CANON` propagates a minor-card line.
Trump events do not belong to that line.

Short formula:

```text
trump breaks transmission
```

---

## 6. Why This Works

`CANON` is strong because it can keep going.

But it only keeps going
while the revealed top card continues to affirm the line.

That gives it:

- variable depth
- real risk
- real continuation law

It is not:

- fixed draw 2
- unrestricted chain draw
- topdeck gambling with no structure

It is doctrinal propagation.

of one recognized line.
